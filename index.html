Ionization/dynamical models of proplyds

Authors

Will Henney and Nahiely Flores.

Components

  • models/ Cylindrically symmetric models of the physical structure of a proplyd (density, velocity, temperature, ionization state, etc), which are created by patching together multiple Cloudy models.

  • emission/ Calculation of PPV (position-position-velocity) cubes of multiple emission lines from the physical models. The PPV cubes can then by sliced and summed to produce spectral line profiles for arbitrary apertures or surface brightness maps.
